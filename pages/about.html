About We Made Fire

Hello fellow Cave Dwellers! Welcome to the website of We Made Fire. I'm excited to introduce myself and speak a little about the future of the project. Orignally this was the Crypto Cavemen project, but I have since brought on 2 new members with skills in marketing and writing. We decided to rebrand to grow a more scalable brand then Crypto Cavemen. The Crypto Cavemen will be a fundamental part of the world as we write the story of the cavemen and Cryptopia. We are taking the project in the direction of story telling with NFT's and are excited to tell the story of the Cavemen and build a living world around these mayhem creating mongrels.

There are only 555 legacy cavemen and that is all that will ever be minted from the original collection. You can purchase one on DeadRare or XOXNO and join the Discord, Ooga Booga and hang out with da other dwellers! The writer will be creating stories for the Cavemen of Cryptopia (More about the new members coming soon)! We will be building out the discord and doing more community related events to build community. There will be a lot coming with the project overtime, but as many projects have over promised and underdelivered, we are not creating a roadmap, but a community and having fun while we do it.

You can stay up to date on the story here on the 'We Made Fire' website, Meet friends and have a good time on discord, or follow us on Twitter to keep up with the latest events. Get yourself a Caveman today and join the community! More to come as the project continues to grow.
