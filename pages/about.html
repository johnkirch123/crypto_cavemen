About We Made Fire

We Made Fire is the next stage in the evolution of Crypto Cavemen—one of the first-ever collections to mint on MultiversX. We’re an NFT collective founded on the idea that no matter how advanced we humans become, we’re all, at heart, an ever-evolving species doing the best we can with the tools we have. Intrigued? Well, gather around the campfire, ’cause in the coming months, we have an all-new story to tell.

John Kirch Jr

A developer, artist, and all-purpose builder, John is a veteran of the military who lives in Atlanta. He’s built a strong skill set in many programming languages like Javascript and Python, before switching to full stack web development UX/UI design. The founder of Crypto Cavemen, John began the collection on MultiversX as a one-person operation in 2021, doing everything from the minting to the drawing himself. He then teamed up with two partners (see below) to rebrand the collection as We Made Fire, a storytelling NFT. In his spare time, John likes to nerd-out on fixing pneumatic tubes and other devices most people can’t pronounce.

Mathieu Flamant

A native of Belgium currently based in Austin, Texas, Matt boasts more than 20 years’ experience in commercial sales and sales leadership. He’s worked with several startups and helped launch numerous market-disrupting medical products. When Matt isn’t on a sales call, he takes care of his four dogs—which is one fewer than the number of languages he speaks. Translation: Matt can talk your ear off about the Dallas Cowboys in French, Flemish, Portuguese, Spanish, and Southern-fried English.

Kevin Capp

Based in Austin, Texas, Kevin is an expert at written communications. As a journalist, he’s reported on everything from gang crime to Vegas nightclubs. As a content writer for a boxing promotions company, he spearheaded social media strategy for the richest fight in history–Mayweather vs. Pacquiao. And as a copywriter, his clients have included Delta, Wells Fargo, and Campbell’s. In addition to dreaming up the lore for We Made Fire, Kevin is directing a documentary about the best writer you’ve never heard of—Grover Lewis.
